Frequently Asked Questions
How long does laundry take? ▼
Most laundry is ready within 24 hours. For bulkier items like duvets, it may take up to 48 hours. We'll let you know when your clothes are ready!
How does pickup & delivery work? ▼
For ₦3,000, we'll come to your location to pick up your clothes and bring them back clean and neatly packaged. Just provide your address when ordering.
How do I pay? ▼
Payment can be made on delivery or via bank transfer. We accept cash and POS payments at our shop. You can also use our Pay Charge Centre.
Do you handle stain removal? ▼
Yes! We offer professional stain removal as part of our service. Contact us first via WhatsApp if you have stubborn stains so we can advise you.
Can I drop off clothes at any time? ▼
We are available 24/7. Feel free to WhatsApp us at any time to arrange a drop-off or pickup, even outside regular hours.
